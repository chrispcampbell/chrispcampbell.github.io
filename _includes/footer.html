About

My name is Chris Campbell. I like pixels. I'm a software developer by day, and when I'm not coding, I'm probably puttering around the neighborhood. Or I'm out taking photos. Or I'm recording music under the pseudonym La Bonne Soupe.

I'm currently employed at Climate Interactive, working on climate simulators like En-ROADS that help people better understand climate change and all the ways we can combat it.

Previously I helped build Plausible Labs, a worker-owned software cooperative here in San Francisco. Before that, I spent 10 years at Sun Microsystems, working on the Java2D and JavaFX graphics engines.

You can find me on Mastodon, on Twitter, or by email at chrispcampbell at gmail dot com.
