About

My name is Chris Campbell. I like pixels. I'm a software developer by day, and when I'm not coding, I'm probably puttering around the neighborhood. Or I'm out taking photos. Or I'm recording music under the pseudonym La Bonne Soupe.

I'm currently employed at Plausible Labs, a worker-owned software cooperative here in San Francisco. Previously I spent 10+ years at Sun Microsystems, working on the Java2D and JavaFX graphics engines.

You can find me on Twitter or by email at chrispcampbell at gmail dot com.
